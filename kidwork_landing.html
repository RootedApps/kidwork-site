How It Works

Simple, safe, and built
for your neighborhood.

From first request to completed job, every step is designed with safety and trust at the center.

1

Families sign up

Parents create a family account and add their child's profile — services, availability, and pricing. Everything is parent-controlled.

2

Neighbors browse

Neighbors search nearby kids by service type. Every neighbor submits a photo ID before they can make a request, keeping a record on file for safety.

3

Parent reviews

Every job request goes to the parent first. They review the neighbor, the job details, and the price before approving anything.

4

Kid gets to work

Once approved, the kid sees the job on their dashboard. Built-in safety check-ins and an emergency button keep everyone connected.

5

Everyone wins

Kids earn real money and build real skills. Families get trusted local help. Neighbors get reliable young talent in their community.

Safety First

Safety isn't a feature.
It's the foundation.

Every decision in KidWork was made with one question in mind: is this safe for kids? Here's how we protect yours.

🛡️

Parent-gated approval

No job is confirmed without a parent's explicit approval. Parents review every request, every detail, before the kid sees it.

🪪

Neighbor ID on file

Every neighbor submits a photo ID before they can make a request. IDs are stored on file — no anonymous hiring on KidWork.

📍

Real-time safety check-ins

Kids can send live check-ins during jobs. Parents receive instant notifications so they're always in the loop.

🚨

Emergency button

Every kid has an "I Need Help" button on their dashboard. One tap alerts their parent immediately.

🔒

Private by design

Neighbors never see a child's last name, phone number, or home address. Contact flows only through parent approval.

💬

Parent-mediated messaging

All communication between neighbors and kids goes through the parent. Kids never communicate directly with neighbors.
